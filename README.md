## **Context and Objective:**  
The analysis focused on identifying sales patterns and customer purchasing behaviour in a supermarket environment. The dataset includes variables such as product category, customer type, quantity sold, city, and total sales revenue.

---

## **Key Variables:**  

- **Sale_id (object):** Unique identifier  
- **Branch (object):** Brand of the supermarket  
- **City (object):** City of the supermarket  
- **Customer_type (object):** Type of customers  
- **Product_name (object):** Name of product  
- **Product_category (object):** Category of product  
- **Quantity (int):** Amount sold  
- **Total_price (float64):** Amount of sales in USD  

---

## **Data Cleaning:**  
Missing values were identified and removed to improve data accuracy and consistency.

---

## **Remove Duplicates:**  
Duplicate transactions were checked using the Sale_id column and removed where necessary to maintain data reliability.

---

## **Format Data:**  
Data formatting was performed to ensure consistency and improve data readability. Numerical variables such as quantity and total sales were formatted as numbers, while categorical variables such as product category and customer type were formatted as text. Proper formatting helps improve data accuracy and supports more reliable analysis and visualization.
